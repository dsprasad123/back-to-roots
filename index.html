Food Diet Plans

Eat consistently. Choose consciously.

Veg

  • 🌅 Morning: Soaked almonds + Lemon water
  • 🍛 Breakfast: Ragi porridge + jaggery
  • 🥗 Lunch: Hand-pounded rice + Dal + Ghee
  • ☕ Evening: Boiled groundnuts + Banana
  • 🌙 Dinner: Khichdi + Curd + Pickle

Non-Veg

  • 🌅 Morning: Tulsi herbal tea + soaked raisins
  • 🍛 Breakfast: Idli + coconut chutney
  • 🥗 Lunch: Brown rice + fish curry + veg curry
  • ☕ Evening: Coconut water + sweet potato
  • 🌙 Dinner: Roti + Egg curry + salad
